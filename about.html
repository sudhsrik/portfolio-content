About Me

The field of Networking and Communications has evolved greatly in recent years fueled by advances in Telecommunications, Artificial Intelligence and perfect interplay between complex hardware and software. This dynamic and pervasive field has always aroused my fascination and has inculcated in me an insatiable desire to learn more. My undergraduate education in Electronics and Communication Engineering gave me an opportunity to obtain a glimpse of the extensive research prospects in this area and motivated me to pursue a MS program in Electrical and Computer Engineering focused on Networking and Communications. This interest is due to the projects and coursework I did as a part of my curriculum.

The emphasis given to hands on learning and the close collaboration between science and engineering greatly influenced my decision to pursue a Masters in ECE at UM. After this program, I plan to join the RnD department of a prominent communication company like Qualcomm, Cisco, etc. and work on research projects that will advance the state of the art in communications and networking. Considering the huge emphasis given to research and innovation, I am confident that this program would go a long way in furthering my career and helping me achieve these goals.
