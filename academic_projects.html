Masters, University of Michigan

Secured Electronic Voting System Using the Concepts of Blockchain (Python, multithreading, TCP/IP, UDP, socket programming)

During my third semester, we developed a novel blockchain algorithm using python to maintain the integrity of the voting data stored in an electronic voting system. In contrast to existing algorithms that involve token transfer between nodes during a transaction, our model stored the vote data inside the transactions itself. We developed our code from scratch instead of reusing architectures from Ethereum and Bitcoin as our algorithm was unique. By learning the concepts from the fundamental principles, we were able to effectively identify the strengths and weaknesses of this technology. This enabled us to devise novel strategies to enhance the efficacy and adequately compensate for the shortcomings of this technology. We presented our results in a research paper at an IEEE international conference. This experience taught me to reason from first principles.

Prefetching and edge caching for CDNs

As a summer intern at IIT, Guwahati, I worked to improve the Quality of Service (QoS) for the end-user in DASH video streaming by implementing prefetching in Content Delivery Networks (CDN). The implementation was done in CDNsim, a simulator with a C++ backend that runs on top of the OMNet++ framework. Based on the predicted future data rate between the mobile device and base station, the appropriate encoded alternative of the subsequent video chunk was fetched in advance from the origin server and cached in the CDN. This increased the cache hit ratio and improved QoS for the end-user by minimizing buffering. Since prefetching was not available, I had to modify the source code to include this functionality. I used fourth-order interpolation technique and formulated an equation expressing the data rate as a function of time. In addition to gaining knowledge on the MPEG-DASH standard, my problem-solving approach became more refined and I acquired a better understanding of the methodology for conducting research.
