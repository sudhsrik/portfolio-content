Research Experience

RL for enabling URLLC over noisy feedback channels for low power short message IoT applications

Investigated the use of Reinforcement Learning (RL) for enhancing the reliability of an instantaneous feedback based variable symbol length scheme. Simulated the above transmission scheme using python and applied RL to enforce a constant packet length in spite of variable symbol lengths per bit. Interim results indicated that use of RL reduced the BER by upto 10X at 5dB SNR compared to using fixed length BPSK symbols.

Estimating Remaining Useful Life of Lithoum Ion batteries by using Digital Twins

Developed an ML based Digital Twin (DT) solution that utilizes RNNs to estimate the Remaining Useful Life (RUL) of Lithium ion batteries with high accuracy and robustness under dynamic operating conditions. Two DTs were used: one for estimating battery capacity and the other for extrapolating it to measure RUL.
